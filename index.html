Looking Ahead

MADS Studio is a student-driven game development team building its identity, experience, and portfolio one project at a time. Our team is focused on growing into a fully realized production studio capable of delivering polished, memorable, and mechanically rich titles.

We continue exploring real industry pipelines: gameplay prototypes, engine workflows, animation systems, AI behavior, lighting, narrative development, and team production structure. Every project helps us sharpen our skills and push our creative identity forward.

Unity Engine

Unity allows us to quickly prototype gameplay, build 2D and 3D hybrids, explore procedural generation, and develop mobile-ready projects with speed and flexibility.

Unreal Engine 5

Unreal gives us access to high-end visuals, cinematic tools, advanced lighting systems, and next-gen development workflows for more ambitious projects.

Our long-term vision is to launch fully playable titles across multiple platforms while building a distinct creative style. From experimental prototypes to narrative experiences, MADS Studio aims to leave a mark in the indie and student development scene — one game at a time.

This is just the beginning.
